✅ TAMA KA! BUNDLES HINDI DAPAT KASAMA!
========================================

🎯 BAKIT?
=========
Bundles = VIRTUAL PRODUCTS lang!
- Bundle ay combination ng existing items
- Yung actual items ay nasa inventory pa rin
- Kaya HINDI dapat kasama sa total value
- Para walang DOUBLE COUNTING!

📊 EXAMPLE:
===========
INVENTORY:
- Item A: 10 pcs × ₱100 = ₱1,000
- Item B: 20 pcs × ₱50  = ₱1,000
TOTAL: ₱2,000

BUNDLE:
- "Bundle AB" = 1× Item A + 2× Item B
- Price: ₱180
- Qty: 5 pcs

❌ MALI (kung kasama bundles):
Inventory: ₱2,000
Bundles:   ₱900 (5 × ₱180)
TOTAL:     ₱2,900 ← DOUBLE COUNTED!

✅ TAMA (inventory lang):
Inventory: ₱2,000
Bundles:   NOT counted
TOTAL:     ₱2,000 ← CORRECT!
