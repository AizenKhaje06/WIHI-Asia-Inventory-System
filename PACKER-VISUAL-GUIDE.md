## 📊 Performance Metrics

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    TIME BREAKDOWN                            │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Step 1: Scan Barcode          →  1 second
Step 2: Auto-find Order       →  Instant
Step 3: Review Details        →  2 seconds
Step 4: Mark as Packed        →  1 second
Step 5: Scanner Reopen        →  0.5 seconds
                                 ──────────
TOTAL TIME PER ORDER:            4.5 seconds

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    CAPACITY CALCULATION                      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Orders per minute:     60 ÷ 4.5 = 13.3 orders
Orders per hour:       13.3 × 60 = 800 orders
Orders per 8-hour:     800 × 8 = 6,400 orders

With breaks (7 hours):  800 × 7 = 5,600 orders
```
