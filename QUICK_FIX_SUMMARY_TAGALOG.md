## Paano I-test?

### Test Return Rate:
1. Pumunta sa Dashboard
2. Tingnan ang "Return Rate" card - dapat may percentage na (hindi 0%)
3. Mag-add ng return sa Inventory > Restock (reason: damaged-return)
4. Refresh dashboard - dapat tumaas ang return rate

### Test Fast/Slow Moving sa PDF:
1. Pumunta sa Business Insights
2. Click "PDF (All Reports)" button
3. Check kung may 8 sections ang PDF
4. Section 4 - dapat may fast moving items
5. Section 5 - dapat may slow moving items

### Test Dead Stock Days to Sell:
1. Pumunta sa Business Insights > Dead Stock tab
2. Check kung may "Days to Sell" column
3. Export PDF
4. Section 7 - dapat may days to sell per item
