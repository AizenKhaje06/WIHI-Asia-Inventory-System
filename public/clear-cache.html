🔧 Clear Service Worker Cache

This tool will clear all cached data from the service worker and force a fresh reload of all images and assets.

Manual Steps (if button doesn't work):

  1. Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete
  2. Select "Cached images and files"
  3. Click "Clear data"
  4. Press Ctrl + Shift + R to hard refresh
  5. Or go to DevTools → Application → Service Workers → Unregister
