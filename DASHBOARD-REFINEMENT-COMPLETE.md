## Subtle Professional Improvements (Hindi OA)

### What Changed

Made **subtle, professional refinements** to improve visual hierarchy and breathing room without being over-the-top.

---

## 🎯 Key Improvements

### 1. Reduced KPI Cards (7 → 5)
**Before:** 7 cards in one row (crowded)
**After:** 5 cards in one row (balanced)

**Removed:**
- Items Sold Today (redundant with Total Sold)
- Cancelled Orders (moved to insights section)

**Kept (Most Important):**
- Total Revenue
- Net Profit
- Total Sold
- Profit Margin
- Inventory Value

### 2. Better Spacing
**Changed:**
- `gap-3` → `gap-4` (more breathing room)
- `p-3` → `p-4` (larger card padding)
- `mb-1.5` → `mb-2` (better vertical rhythm)

### 3. Improved Visual Hierarchy
**Card Sizes:**
- Icon containers: `p-1.5` → `p-2` (more prominent)
- Icon sizes: `h-3 w-3` → `h-4 w-4` (clearer)
- Numbers: `text-xl` → `text-2xl` (bolder)
- Labels: `text-xs` → `text-sm` (more readable)

### 4. Subtle Hover Effects
**Added:**
- `hover:shadow-lg transition-shadow` on all cards
- Smooth shadow transitions on hover
- Professional, not flashy

### 5. Cleaner Section Headers
**Simplified:**
- "Enhanced Metric Cards - 7 KPIs" → "Key Metrics - 5 Primary KPIs"
- "Quick Stats Row - 4 Mini Cards" → "Secondary Metrics - 4 Cards"
- "Performance Charts - 3 Horizontal Bar Charts" → "Performance Analytics"
- "Distribution Charts - 2 Vertical Bar Charts" → "Stock Distribution"
- "Recent Activity - 2 Lists" → "Recent Activity"
- "Business Insights & Inventory Health - NEW" → "Insights & Health"
